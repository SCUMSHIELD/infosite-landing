About Our Project

Our innovative artificial intelligence system is designed to proactively identify and mitigate risks that threaten our children and communities. By utilizing advanced techniques such as honeypots and youthful actors, we aim to create a safer environment for our youth.

The Need for Our Project

In today's world, children face numerous dangers, including online predators, cyberbullying, and violence. Current laws often fall short in addressing these issues. Our project seeks to fill this gap by acting before threats escalate, ensuring that our children are protected.
